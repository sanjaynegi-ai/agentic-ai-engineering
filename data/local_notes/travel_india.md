# India travel notes
## Jaipur
Amber Fort is best visited early. City Palace and Jantar Mantar fit well together. Budget for local transport and keep a weather fallback; monsoon showers can be sudden.
## Delhi
Cluster Old Delhi separately from India Gate and the museum district. Metro travel reduces road-delay risk.
## Mumbai
Group Colaba sights together. Coastal humidity and monsoon rain affect walking plans.
## Bengaluru
Traffic buffers matter. Lalbagh, Cubbon Park, and central museums suit a relaxed plan.
## Goa
North and South Goa are far enough apart to plan as separate day clusters. Follow local sea-safety warnings.
